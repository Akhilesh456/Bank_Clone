
This is a banking system!!

It can perform 7 functions, 
1) Opening an accounts
    User has to provide Name, chosen PIN, and starting money.
their account will be opened. This banking system keeps track of the accounts created using
text files. So even if the programme finishes running, bank 
accounts are stored. 

2) Deposite money into account
    Pretty self explanatory. You enter the money, account number
and money gets deposited into the account.

3) Withdraw money from account
    Enter, account number, PIN, and money and it gets withdrawn
from the account. Won't withdraw if asking money is greater
than the money in the account.

4) Transfer money
    You can transfer money from one account to another

5) Shows account details

6) Shows the entire transaction history
